New Game Room Opens in Room D208

The school is pleased to announce the opening of a new game room in Room D208 (Primary Containers). This space provides students with opportunities to enjoy games that encourage creativity, problem-solving, and strategic thinking, while also offering a positive environment for collaboration.

December 5, 2025

Features

  • Strategy-based board games that promote planning and foresight
  • Creative puzzles that challenge imagination and innovation
  • Team-oriented activities that strengthen communication and cooperation

Rules of Use

  • Respect all equipment and return games after use
  • Food and drinks are not permitted inside
  • Keep noise levels appropriate
  • Follow the instructions of supervising teachers at all times

Supervision and Schedule

The game room will only be open during designated times when teachers are present to supervise. A schedule of supervising staff is posted at the entrance of Room D208, and students should check it regularly to plan their visits.

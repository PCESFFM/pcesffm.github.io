Clash Royale Tournament Announced by Sports Committee

Competitive 1v1 play with a €25 prize

November 8

The Sports Committee is organizing a Clash Royale Tournament, offering students an opportunity for competitive 1v1 play and the chance to win a €25 prize.

This tournament is structured around 1v1 matches, each with a duration limit of 30 minutes. The format is designed to be accessible and engaging, providing a direct competitive environment for all participants. The primary objective is to crown a single winner who will receive the monetary prize.

Key Tournament Details

  • Format: 1v1 Matches
  • Match Duration: 30 Minutes
  • Prize: €25
  • Entry Fee: Free (No cost to enter)
  • Sign-Up Limit: None (No maximum number of participants)
  • Registration Type: Solo sign-up only

Registration and Eligibility

The tournament is open to all students, and the organizing committee has ensured there are no maximum limitations on the number of sign-ups, promoting broad participation. Due to the nature of the prize, all participants must register individually, with only solo entries being accepted.

Interested participants have two accessible methods for signing up for the event:

  • Online Registration: Sign up using the link provided in the ESFFM Instagram bio.
  • In-Person Registration: Register in Room C109 during the 20-minute break on Wednesday.

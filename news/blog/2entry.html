Drawing Competition

S1–S3 pupils explore “home” and unity through art

November 8

Our school recently held a drawing competition for S1–S3 pupils, encouraging students to explore what “home” and unity mean to them through art. The event aimed to give young artists the chance to express their thoughts and feelings in creative and personal ways.

The response was fantastic, with many impressive entries that showed a wide range of styles, ideas, and techniques. Some students focused on their home countries, while others used global themes to show how people everywhere are connected. Each piece reflected the individuality and imagination of its creator, making it a real display of student talent.

The winning entry immediately captures attention using its powerful imagery with the world at its centre, surrounded by three-dimensional houses with glowing lights shining through. Behind them a background of fire adds depth and contrast, suggesting both warmth and danger. The artwork delivers a striking message about connection and responsibility, reminding people that even in times of crisis, we all share one home.

Congratulations to the winner for their outstanding work, and to all the participants for making the competition such a success. The talent and imagination shown by our S1–S3 pupils truly made this event one to remember.

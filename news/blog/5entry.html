Pajama Day

A cosy and colourful school-wide event!

Febuary 27, 2026

Our school recently held a pajama day on the 18th of December, where the students came to school in cosy pyjamas and comfortable outfits. Classrooms were filled with colourful patterns and smiling faces, creating a relaxed and lively atmosphere.

Many students joined in with a variety of pyjamas, from onesies to patterned sets, while others even wore slippers or brought cuddly toys. Seeing everyone dressed so comfortably brought a sense of fun to the school and made the day feel a little different from usual.

It was wonderful to see the students enjoying the chance to express themselves while spending time with friends. Pajama day brought the school community together and added a light-hearted touch to the end of term.

The Team

A big thank you to everyone who helped organise and promote Pajama Day. Your enthusiasm and participation helped create a warm, welcoming, and memorable event for the entire school community.

We look forward to bringing you more fun and engaging activities throughout the school year!

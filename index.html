This is the official European School Frankfurt Pupils' Committee Website!

We are a small team of students, elected by you with the mission to improve all aspects of school life for everyone. We achieve this goal by organising parties, school clean-ups and other projects, with the help of sub-committees managed by us, i.e. the green committee, the events committee, etc.
Scroll down to learn more!

Committees

The Pupils' Committee is divided into 3 Main committees

Green Committee

The Green Committee leads our school's sustainability efforts — tending the school garden, organising clean-up days, watering study room plants, and championing eco-friendly initiatives that keep our campus clean and green.

Events Committee

The Events Committee brings school life to life! They plan and run everything from end-of-year parties and summer festivals to charity drives and community gatherings — making sure there's always something to look forward to.

Media Committee

The Media Committee manages our school's online presence. From Instagram posts to TikTok videos, they keep the student body informed and engaged — documenting events, sharing news, and giving our school a voice on social media.
